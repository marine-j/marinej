Increase your productivity and beat procrastination !

The pomodoro technique is a great technique to help you work better. The simple idea is to set a timer (usually for 25 minutes but you can adapt) and to completly focus on your work during this time : no web surfing, no emails, no games etc...

This helps you with procrastination because it makes you feel that 25 minutes of work is totally doable :)

Congratulations ! You just finished a pomodoro !!!

It is important to reward yourself and take a break now, this will help you link good feelings to the work you just done and will make you a little bit more enclined to work again next time. Breaks are also an important part of the memorization process.

See Quora article on the importance of breaks »

Before you start a new pomodoro, you should take the time to recall what you just learned during your previous pomodoro. When recalling, you should not be reading your materials (books, website...), only remembering the main ideas and the key concepts.

Recall makes the learning process much much more effective than simply re-reading your material.
